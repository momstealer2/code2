Our Story

Jewellery Made With Love & Purpose

We believe that every woman deserves to shine — every single day, not just on special occasions.

Who We Are

Born from a Passion for Everyday Elegance

MERSSH Jewellery was founded with a single dream — to make premium, long-lasting jewellery accessible to every woman across India. We grew tired of watching beautiful pieces lose their lustre after just a few wears.

So we set out to source the finest anti-tarnish Korean jewellery — lightweight, waterproof, and stunning — that you can wear to the office, the gym, or a candlelit dinner without a second thought.

From our very first collection, we knew we had something special. Today, over 1,000 happy customers trust MERSSH to elevate their everyday style. And we're just getting started.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Every piece we create, every order we ship, and every customer we serve is guided by these principles.

Quality First

We never compromise on materials. Every piece undergoes strict quality checks to ensure it meets our anti-tarnish and durability standards.

Made for Real Women

Our designs are inspired by real women — their routines, their style, and their desire to feel beautiful without the fuss of delicate upkeep.

Fair Pricing

Premium jewellery shouldn't cost a fortune. We keep our margins honest so that luxury is accessible to every budget.

Fast & Safe Delivery

We pack every order with care, ensuring your jewellery arrives pristine and ready to wear — backed by our delivery guarantee.

Eco-Conscious

From packaging to production, we're committed to reducing our footprint and making choices that are kinder to our planet.

Customer Delight

Our support team is always here for you. Your satisfaction is not just a metric — it's the reason we show up every day.
